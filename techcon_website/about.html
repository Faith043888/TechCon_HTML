History

TechCon began in 2015 with the vision of creating a platform for developers, creators, and tech enthusiasts to come together and exchange knowledge. Over the years, it has grown into one of the most anticipated technology conferences in the world.

Past Speakers

  • Dr. Ada Lovelace – AI Pioneer
  • Elon Dev – Founder, DevRocket Inc.
  • Grace Hopper Jr. – Cybersecurity Expert

Why Attend?

Whether you're just starting out or you're an industry veteran, TechCon offers something for everyone — from hands-on workshops to keynote presentations and networking opportunities.
